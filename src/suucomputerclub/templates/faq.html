Frequently Asked Questions


What do I have to know about computers to join?

Nothing! We learn about computers and related subjects at the Computer Club. novice to laureate, all are welcome.

What do you do at Computer Club?

We discuss and work on matter related to computers. This is vague on purpose. We may design a website, hear from a speaker or discuss what is new and exciting in the broad field. It is very open so we can keep gain a broad understanding of all that falls into our digital world.

How do I join?

The requirements a very simple, attend a few meetings and want to be a member. Do those two things, and you've joined.

Where do you meet?

We meet every Tuesday at 4:00pm in ELC 310.

Who do I contact for more information?

You can contact us at suu.computerclub@gmail.com

Do I need to attend every meeting?

No, though we will hunt you down if you don't. ;-)
